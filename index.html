About Us

Our Story

Tomatoes Bazar is a family business with a big heart. Founded by a father of five with more than 30 years of retail experience across four countries, our vision is simple: bring families together around good food at great prices.

Our logo and brand were created with love — a collective effort of family and friends — reflecting who we are: warm, welcoming, and fresh.

Our Mission

To provide Milton's families with the freshest produce, quality halal meats, and baked goods, all while creating a new and exciting shopping experience.

Our Vision

To become the go-to neighborhood supermarket where quality meets affordability and every customer feels at home.

Family-Owned

Built on 30+ years of retail experience with a focus on community, quality, and value.

Freshness & Quality

Handpicked produce and quality products that bring healthy meals to your table.

Trust & Honesty

We believe in building lasting relationships with our community through transparency.

Affordability

Making fresh, quality food accessible to every family with unbeatable prices.
