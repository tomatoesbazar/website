Our Story

A Family Business
with a Big Heart

Founded by a father of five with more than 30 years of retail experience across four countries, our vision is simple: bring families together around good food at great prices.

Our logo and brand were created with love — a collective effort of family and friends — reflecting who we are: warm, welcoming, and fresh.
